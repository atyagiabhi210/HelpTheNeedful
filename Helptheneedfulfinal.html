HELLO USERS!

Welcome to help the needful website.

This website offers you to help the needful or to get help if you are in need. Our vision is simple, all we want is people who want to give something to the needful provide information here and the needful to get information and help. Hence, both are benefitted.

Why to help the needful:

It is said “Those who are gracious to the poor lend to the Lord, and the Lord will fully repay them.” In many ways, the mere act of being charitable is its own reward. But as this verse notes, being compassionate to people who are less fortunate is also something that God will reward. This platform provides you to help the needful in any form.

How we provide help to the needful:

You just have to provide the information about what you want to give as charity and users will be able to see your info in the needful section and genuine needful people can then get help by you by filling a short form.

How do we know if our help is going to correct hands:

To check whether the help is going to genuine needful people , the needful are required to fill a form first. In the form necessary details like name , address, e.t.c are asked. More over needful are required to attach a proof that they want help. For ex if they want help as oxygen cylinder or blood , they would have to attach hospital bill and other necessary info.

Do we get something by helping people:

Well apart from getting the blessings of people and god, use also get some points every time you donate something which can be used to get exciting rewards.
